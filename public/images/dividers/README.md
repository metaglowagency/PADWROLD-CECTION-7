
# Institutional Asset Repository: Dividers

Place institutional-grade background images for the parallax dividers here.

## Required Asset List
To maintain the dashboard's visual integrity, please provide high-resolution images (min 2560px width) with the following filenames:

- `stack.jpg`: Abstract architecture or vertical structures.
- `economics.jpg`: Mathematical precision or data visualization.
- `scale.jpg`: Global networking or satellite imagery.
- `roi.jpg`: High-end financial charts or abstract growth.
- `fastpad.jpg`: Speed, kinetic energy, or high-tech motion.
- `share.jpg`: Transparency, glass, or split-screen concepts.
- `compound.jpg`: Fibonacci spirals or repeating geometric patterns.
- `risk.jpg`: Shielding, encryption, or solid foundations.

## Specifications
- **Format**: `.jpg` (optimized for web)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or wider
- **Aesthetic**: Minimalist, dark, high-contrast.
